About

I’ve been playing piano for ten years, and started teaching in early 2018. My instructing style is similar to the Bastien Method; I teach sight reading from the beginning, and introduce increasingly more difficult sheet music as the student progresses.

I always have my students play the staple classical pieces, but believe that letting them play music they enjoy - whether it be from TV, movies, or video games - is vital for growing a love of piano.

I’ve taught beginners, as well as kids who are already at an intermediate or advanced level. I also have experience working with children who have special needs.
